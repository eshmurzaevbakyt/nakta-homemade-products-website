Our story

Born from a mother's kitchen

When I moved to the United States, I missed more than just home. I missed the aroma of freshly made manty, the taste of real homemade meals — the feeling you simply can't find in a store.

That's how NAKTA was born. Every item is carefully made with clean, high-quality ingredients. No artificial colors. No additives. Non-GMO. 100% Halal.

Just real food. Just like home. Just like mom's.

"NAKTA is more than food — it's a feeling. A connection to home, to childhood, and to real, honest cooking."
